About

WEI is community base organization that is working to help poor and disadvantage women, young girls and children restore their lost hope, we work different society such as ; patriarchal communities where women are oppressed voiceless in decision making ,have no access to opportunities and resources . WEI empower women economically, socially and political to enable women and young girls to achieve gender equality and self-development.

NON-discriminations

COVID-19 in Uganda

On March 22nd, Uganda's health officials confirmed the first case of COVID-19 in Uganda. Although the border regions take the brunt of COVID-19 cases in Uganda, it has come with numbers of effect on the life of women and young girls in the communities such as increase level of gender based violence ,teenage pregnancy , continue to struggle to have their basic needs met ,limited educational resources and school materials. In July, as lockdown began to relax, the effects of the pandemic pressed on. Many families suffering from different type of violence or being abuse, have completely lost their livelihoods and saving , have do not have access to proper learning materials and lack adequate health resources and information. These harmful effects most severely impact women and girls. As WEI we are supporting survivors of violence with psycho-social support.
